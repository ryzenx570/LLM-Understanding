Layer 0 — The Word Breaker

Everything starts as tiny bricks!

An LLM can't read a whole sentence at once. It has to smash words into "Tokens" (Lego bricks). A short word like "Cat" is one single brick. But a long, scary word like "Neurotransmitter" gets smashed into 4 or 5 tiny pieces!

Layer 1 — The Color & Map

Bricks need flavor and a GPS!

[ INSERT ANIMATION: Bricks being dipped in color ]

If all bricks were just grey, the AI wouldn't know the difference between "Apple" and "Dog." We dip every brick in a special color (Embedding) so it has a "flavor." Then we add a sticker (Positioning) so the AI knows which brick comes first!

Layer 3 — The Spotlight

The Flashlight Trick!

[ INSERT ANIMATION: A brick shining a beam on another ]

When the AI reads a sentence, it uses "Attention." This is like having a flashlight. One brick can shine its light on another brick to say: "Hey, you are important to me!" This helps the AI connect ideas.
